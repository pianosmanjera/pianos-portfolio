// 01 — About

Turning complex data
into decision-ready insight

I'm a GIS and data analytics professional with over nine years of experience supporting humanitarian and development operations across Southern and Eastern Africa, and Afghanistan. I specialize in transforming data into decision-ready insights for food security analysis, targeting, emergency preparedness, and programme monitoring.

My work integrates geospatial analysis, dashboards, survey systems, and analytics to support both operational teams and strategic planning. I've contributed to regional and country-level initiatives, supporting UN agencies and partners with vulnerability assessments, hazard monitoring, and information management systems.

I work across the full data and analysis pipeline — from collection and quality assurance to spatial modelling, dashboard development, reporting, and decision support. I enjoy building solutions that translate complex data into practical, usable intelligence for operational teams and leadership.

My areas of interest include food security analysis, hazard monitoring, GIS-enabled decision support, digital systems improvement, and the use of analytics to strengthen accountability and programme effectiveness.
